Arizona

Arizona has a couple different terrains. In northern Arizona, there are pine trees and forests and snow in the winter. There is also plains with inactive volcanoes in another part of the state. The part of Arizona that I live in is just desert with cacti everywhere. In the summers, it reaches up to 118 degrees F and in the winters, it only gets down to about 40 degrees F. It is a great place to live.
